Question8: Two point Charges in air at a distance of 20 cm, from each other interact with a certain force. At what distance from each other should these charges be placed in oil of relative permittivity 5 to obtain the same force of interaction

a) $8.94 \times 10^{-2}$ m

b) $0.894 \times 10^{-2}$ m

c) $89.4 \times 10^{-2}$ m

d) $8.94 \times 10^{-2}$ m

Solution

Given r = 0.2 m, $ ϵ_{r} $= 5
Force between two charges in air
$ F_{air} =\frac{q_{1} q_{2}}{4 \pi \epsilon_{0}r^{2}}$
Force between two charges in oil of relative permittivity $ϵ_{r} = 5$
$ F_{oil}=\frac{ q_{1} q_{2}}{4\pi \epsilon_{oil}r_{oil}^{2}}$
$ F_{oil}=\frac{ q_{1} q_{2}}{4\pi \epsilon_{0}\epsilon_{r}r_{oil}^{2}}$
We know $ϵ_{oil}=ϵ_{0} ϵ_{r}$
$F_{air} = F_{oil}$
$r_{oil}^{2} = \frac{r^{2}}{ϵ_{r}} = 8.94 × 10^{-2}m$
